Year Up Bay Area: Helping Close the Opportunity Divide

Year Up was founded in Boston, Massachusetts in the year 2000. Year Up's mission is to "to close the Opportunity Divide by providing urban young adults with the skills, experience, and support that will empower them to reach their potential through professional careers and higher education." After a tough 6 months of technical training, students earn an internship at a Fortune 500 company. Students are supported throughout their journey and even ear college credits during the training period.


120 students are accepted every 6 months for the San Francisco Bay Area Year Up location in downtown SF. Located in the middle of the Financial District, Year Up is in a prime location for young adults to become familiarized with the atmosphere of corporate America.
