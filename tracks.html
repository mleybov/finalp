Tracks That Year Up Offers:




Cyber Security, IT/Help Desk, QA (Quality Assurance), Project Management, and Network/System Administration.

Students are placed into different tracks depending on interest. They can earn an internship seat position that will allow them to utilize what they learned in their specific track. From learning IT/help desk to software testing in the QA track, students will be exposed to many possible entry level positions for their future. Having such a large variety of possible fields allows students to explore different tracks to make sure that what they will be placed in is something that they enjoy and are truely interested in.

There are 2 phases of the Year Up Experience:
The first 5 months are called the L&D phase
L&D stands for learning and development.
The next 6 months, that follow the L&D phase, are the internship months that the student will spend working at their internship company.
